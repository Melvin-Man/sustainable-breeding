Sustainable Breeding

A tool to explore best practices in animal production.

Breed Selection

Choosing the right breed is a strategic decision that impacts productivity and sustainability. This section allows you to compare breeds based on their purpose and climate adaptation, a key factor for optimizing the use of local resources and profitability.

Life Cycle

Understanding growth stages is vital for efficient management. Explore the key phases from birth to maturity to optimize nutrition, health, and production planning.

Balanced Nutrition

Feed represents the highest production cost. Here you can visualize nutritional requirements by species and stage, key to maximizing feed efficiency and profitability.

Infrastructure and Space

Proper facility design is essential for welfare and productivity. This section details the space requirements to prevent stress and diseases, which translates into better performance.

Health and Prevention

Prevention is the foundation of a healthy and profitable herd. Explore the most common diseases and key preventive measures. Click on a disease to see more details.

Impact and Sustainability

Sustainable livestock farming seeks to align profitability with environmental responsibility. Learn about the impact of production and key practices to mitigate it, such as waste management and efficient water use.

Production and Performance

Efficiency is the engine of profitability. This section presents key performance indicators, such as weight gain and feed conversion, which are fundamental for a successful operation.
